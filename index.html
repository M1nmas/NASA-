Stellar Stories: Space Weather Through the Eyes of Earthlings

Introduction to Space Weather

Space weather refers to the dynamic and variable conditions in the space environment that affect Earth and its technological systems.

Chemical Reactions on the Sun

The Sun's core is where nuclear fusion occurs, primarily through the proton-proton chain reaction.

Effects of Space Weather on Earth

  • Disruption of satellite communications
  • Power grid fluctuations
  • Enhanced aurora displays
